OUR PHILOSOPHY

Where Food Meets Symphony

Tamas By Symphony was born out of a simple idea: creating a premium, warm dining escape for food lovers and families in Hisar.

Crafting Premium Flavors

We believe that a great meal has the power to bring people together. That's why we source fresh, high-quality ingredients for all our signature recipes. Each dish is prepared to order, capturing authentic Italian and Japanese flavors, and served with premium care by our passionate culinary team.

But great food is only half our story. In a busy world, we wanted to build a relaxing sanctuary. A place where you can unwind in comfortable leather armchairs, dine under warm lights, and spend hours enjoying the company of loved ones in a peaceful indoor lounge or beautiful open-air garden.

We don't rush you. Whether you enjoy a signature mocktail, a fresh wood-fired pizza, or a delicious Lotus Biscoff cheesecake, your table is yours for as long as you wish to relax and dine.

COZY SPACES

Designed for Your Day

Our restaurant features four distinct dining zones, each tailored to different frames of mind.

The Vintage Lounge

Flanked by elegant wood trims and warm lighting. Features cozy leather armchairs, ambient candles, and a vintage aesthetic.

Open-Air Garden Patio

Our beautiful outdoor patio featuring starry hanging lights, natural greenery, and comfortable seating. Ideal for fresh air dining.

The Family Dining Hall

Spacious main dining hall with plush seating, warm tables, and premium service. A welcoming space to dine with family and friends.

QUALITY GUARANTEE
