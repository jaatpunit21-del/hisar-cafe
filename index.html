THE ATMOSPHERE

A Cozy Vintage Lounge & Kitchen

Warm, cozy, and beautifully designed with vintage elements. Tamas By Symphony was built for those who appreciate great food and slow, relaxing evenings. Complete with cozy indoor couches and a charming open-air garden patio, it's the ideal spot to enjoy a meal with family, catch up with friends, or host a private gathering.

Our chefs take hospitality seriously. From wood-fired Neapolitan pizzas to hand-rolled sushi, signature burgers, and artisanal mocktails, everything is prepared with passion and served with premium care.
